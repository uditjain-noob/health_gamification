MECHANISM:
The liver is the body's largest internal organ, helping to digest food, store energy, and remove poisons. Liver diseases can be caused by viruses (hepatitis), drugs, poisons, or excess alcohol, which can lead to conditions like fatty liver disease and cirrhosis. These diseases impair the liver's ability to perform its essential metabolic and detoxification functions.

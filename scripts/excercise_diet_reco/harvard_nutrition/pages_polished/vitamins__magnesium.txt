CONDITION: Magnesium deficiency, hypomagnesemia
BIOMARKERS: Magnesium, Calcium, Potassium
ORGAN: metabolic
CATEGORY: diet, supplement

---

MECHANISM:
Magnesium is an essential mineral needed for more than 300 biochemical reactions in the body. It helps maintain normal nerve and muscle function, supports a healthy immune system, keeps the heartbeat steady, helps bones remain strong, and aids in adjusting blood glucose levels. Diets high in protein, calcium, or vitamin D will increase the body's need for magnesium.

DIETARY RECOMMENDATIONS:
- Dark green, leafy vegetables
- Fruits such as bananas, dried apricots, and avocados
- Nuts such as almonds and cashews
- Peas, beans (legumes), and seeds
- Soy products such as soy flour and tofu
- Whole grains such as brown rice and millet
- Milk

EXERCISE RECOMMENDATIONS:
General moderate exercise (150 min/week aerobic + 2x strength training) is recommended for overall metabolic health.

SUPPLEMENT RECOMMENDATIONS:
N/A

WARNINGS:
- Symptoms of moderate deficiency include numbness, tingling, muscle contractions, cramps, seizures, personality changes, and abnormal heart rhythms.
- Severe deficiency can cause low blood calcium level (hypocalcemia) and low blood potassium level (hypokalemia).
- Magnesium excess, most often from supplements or laxatives, can occur in people with significantly reduced kidney function.
